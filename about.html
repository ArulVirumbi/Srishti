Shristi 2K21

SRISHTI is an annual technical symposium organised by IEEE Students' Chapter 12951 of PSG College of Technology. Going by the phrase, "unleash the talent within" , Srishti brings out the creme de la creme of the student's community through an diverse array of events. The Symposium is home to multiple technical and non technical events, workshops project and paper presentations. Explore your interests and elevate your skills.

IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global condition
